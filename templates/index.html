MolSimAI: AI based Molecular Comparison and Heatmap Visualization

Welcome to MolSimAI, a cutting-edge tool designed for advanced molecular similarity analysis using AI-driven algorithms.

This application allows users to upload molecular datasets (SMILES format) and receive insights in the form of a similarity matrix and heatmap visualizations.

After analysis, download the similarity matrix and visualize the results in a heatmap, which is ideal for identifying chemical patterns, structural similarities, and exploring molecular diversity.
